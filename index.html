About Us

Introduction: Flipkart is one of India’s leading e-commerce platforms, known for revolutionizing the way Indians shop online. Founded in 2007 by Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal (not related), two former Amazon employees, Flipkart started as an online bookstore and quickly expanded into a full-fledged marketplace selling everything from electronics and fashion to home essentials and groceries.

Flipkart has been instrumental in shaping India's e-commerce landscape by introducing customer-centric innovations such as Cash on Delivery, No Cost EMI, and quick returns. The company continues to focus on expanding its product offerings, enhancing customer experience, and supporting small businesses and entrepreneurs through various initiatives.
